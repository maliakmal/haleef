About Haleef -Your Ally in Business Events and Strategic Networking

Launched in 2025, Haleef stands for Ally or Companion in Arabic - reflecting our mission to build strong, purpose-driven partnerships through professional events. With over 30 years of combined industry experience, our team brings proven expertise in organizing impactful B2B events tailored for markets in the Middle East and Africa. We create curated knowledge-sharing platforms that empower business leaders, institutions, and decision-makers to stay ahead in rapidly evolving industries.

From concept to execution, Haleef delivers results-driven event experiences that inform, engage, and inspire.
